Hi, I'm Rainer. I create software for working with data.

I specialize in the design of tools and user interfaces that help people explore, analyze, present and manage data more easily. I am passionate about maps, and in many of my projects, I have dealt with spatial or cartographic data.

I am also the maintainer of the Annotorious and RecogitoJS open source libraries for text and image annotation, and the lead developer of the award-winning Recogito annotation platform.
